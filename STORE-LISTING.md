### EN
OCHE is the fast, account-free dart scoreboard that keeps everything offline on your device. No sign-up, no ads, no fuss — just open and throw.

**9 game modes**
• x01 (301/501/701) with sets, legs, double in/out, checkout suggestions and bust detection
• Cricket (Standard, Cut-Throat, No Score)
• Around the Clock
• Bob's 27
• Killer
• Shanghai
• Count Up
• Halve It
• Checkout training

**More than a counter**
• A Dart Robot to play against, with adjustable strength
• Average-based handicap — fair games against stronger or weaker opponents
• Per-player statistics & charts, plus match history
• Sound and voice calls (180!, finish, bust)
• Four themes
• Share results and export/import your data

Everything is stored locally. You stay in control.
